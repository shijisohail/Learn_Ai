Pillar map

Where each pillar shows up

Product AI product creation

  • Days: 6 (production), 9 (providers/cost), 20 (observability), 25 (auth/tenancy), 29 & 30 (architecture capstones)
  • Deep dives: From idea to shipped AI product
Stretch: discovery interviews, pricing experiments, roadmap prioritization (RICE), sales/demo narrative for AI features.

Agents AI agent development

  • Days: 2–5 (tools, RAG, LangGraph, multi-agent), 12 (advanced RAG), 13 (CrewAI)
  • Deep dives: Evals, MCP, A2A, Scheduled & event-driven agents
Stretch: formal HITL review queues, multi-tenant agent isolation at scale, custom tool marketplaces.

DS / ML Data science & machine learning

  • Days: 15–21 (PyTorch, HF, LoRA, MLflow, MLOps, observability)
  • Deep dives: Fine-tuning, Tabular ML & NLP pipelines, Experimentation & causal thinking
Stretch: deep entity-resolution systems, uplift modeling, Bayesian A/B, drift detection for tabular + LLM features.
